Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our fleet and booking process.

What is included in the bus rental price?

Our standard rental price includes the bus, an experienced professional driver, and fuel for the specified route. Tolls, parking fees, and driver's meals/accommodation are typically excluded unless you opt for our "All-In" package.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak holiday seasons or long weekends, to guarantee the availability of your preferred fleet type.

Can I request special amenities on board?

Absolutely! We can accommodate requests for special catering, specific movie playlists on the AVOD systems, or onboard tour guides. Please mention your requests in the "Additional Details" section when booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14 days prior to departure are eligible for a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 7-13 days incur a 50% fee, and cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable. Please ask our team via WhatsApp for full terms.
